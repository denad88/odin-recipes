Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2-4 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Sour Cream (or cottage cheese, yogurt)

Directions

  1. Turn on stovetop, add 1 tsp butter and mix
  2. Crack eggs directly into skillet
  3. Continuously mix the eggs and cycle through taking skillet off and on stovetop
  4. After a minute, add salt and pepper
  5. When eggs soft/creamy consistency, turn off stovetop and add sour cream, cottage cheese or yogurt
  6. Place eggs on plate and serve!
